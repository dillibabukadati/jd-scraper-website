About JD Scraper Pro

JD Scraper Pro is a Chrome browser extension that helps businesses and researchers extract business listing data from JustDial.com for market research, lead generation, and competitive analysis.

üéØ Purpose of This Application

This extension extracts publicly available business information from JustDial search results, including business names, addresses, phone numbers, ratings, and review counts. The extracted data can be exported to CSV files or directly to Google Sheets for easy organization and analysis.

üîê Why We Need Google Drive Access

JD Scraper Pro requests access to Google Drive (drive.file scope) to enable the "Export to Google Sheets" feature. This permission allows the extension to:

  • Create new spreadsheet files in your Google Drive with the scraped business data
  • Organize exports in a dedicated "JustDial Scraper Data" folder
  • Format spreadsheets with headers, colors, and auto-sized columns for readability

Important: The extension uses the drive.file scope, which means it can only access files it creates. It cannot read, modify, or delete your existing Google Drive files or spreadsheets.

üìä How Your Data is Used

All data processing happens locally in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any scraped data to external servers. When you export to Google Sheets, the data goes directly from your browser to your Google Drive account using Google's secure APIs. For complete details, please review our Privacy Policy.
